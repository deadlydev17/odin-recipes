Capcay Goreng Bakso Recipe

Cap Cay, or Cap Cai, is a typical Chinese-Indonesian dish made of a medley of stir-fried vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 100 g white cabbage
  • 150 g carrots
  • 150 g broccoli
  • 100 g cauliflower
  • 100 g chicory
  • 100 g bok choy
  • 15 beef meatballs, quartered
  • 50 g peanuts
  • 60 g onions, sliced 1 cm lengthwise
  • 30 g garlic, roughly chopped
  • 30 g shallots, thinly sliced
  • 1 red tomato, cut into 6
  • 19 g oyster sauce
  • 10 g chicken powder
  • 8 g sugar
  • 4 g white pepper powder
  • 17 g cornstarch, dissolved in 50 ml of water
  • 4 g sesame oil
  • 19 g sesame oil
  • 19 g tomato sauce
  • 200 ml water

Directions

  1. Cut vegetables to desired size and set aside.
  2. Parboil cabbage, carrots, broccoli, cauliflower and set aside.
  3. In a pan over medium heat, saut√© shallots and onions until fragrant and lightly brown.
  4. Add water, oyster sauce, chicken powder, sugar, white pepper and tomato sauce. Bring it to a boil.
  5. Add meatballs and stir well.
  6. Add all vegetables in and give it a good stir.
  7. Continue to simmer for another 15 minutes.
  8. Add cornstarch slurry and stir well.
  9. Drizzle sesame oil over, stir and remove.
  10. Garnish with fried onions and serve.
